## The Problem

### Empty seats.  
### Slow marketing.  
### No real-time tools.

Every day, cultural events and restaurants operate below capacity. Seats remain empty, tables go unfilled, and marketing tools are too slow to react when demand stalls.

Social media posts and paid ads are not real-time, not location-based, and not designed for immediate conversion.

The result is structural inefficiency: supply and demand exist at the same moment — but they don’t meet fast enough.

---

## The Solution

Yazzo closes the loop between publishing, booking, and on-site validation.

### 1. Publish  
Venues create real-time offers.

### 2. Discover  
Nearby users see them on a live map and reserve instantly.

### 3. Redeem  
Reservations are validated on-site via QR code or 6-digit code.

Yazzo operates through a three-app ecosystem:
- Consumer app for real-time discovery and reservations  
- Partner app for publishing and managing offers and companies  
- Staff app for on-site validation  

All apps run on a shared real-time backend infrastructure.
