# Yazzo.io Oy  
## Pre-Seed Round Plan  
### Focused Launch: Helsinki

---

# 1. Round Overview

Yazzo is raising a €60–90K pre-seed round to fund 6–9 months of focused launch and validation in Helsinki.

The purpose of this round is not aggressive scaling.  
The purpose is to validate local liquidity, unit economics, and partner retention in a concentrated urban area.

This is a proof round.

---

# 2. Why Helsinki First

Helsinki offers:

- High density of restaurants and cultural venues
- Walkable urban districts
- Strong cultural ecosystem
- Existing founder network
- Early adopter audience

The launch will focus on one micro-area (district cluster) to create real marketplace density before expanding city-wide.

Liquidity is local. Density matters more than total numbers.

---

# 3. Problem

Cultural events and restaurants operate below capacity daily.

When ticket sales slow down or tables remain empty:

- Marketing is slow and reactive
- Paid ads are expensive and unpredictable
- No real-time, location-based activation tool exists

Supply and demand exist at the same moment — but they don’t meet fast enough.

This is a structural inefficiency in urban economies.

---

# 4. The Solution

Yazzo is a real-time capacity layer for cities.

Cultural venues and restaurants publish offers.  
People nearby reserve instantly.  
Redemption is verified on-site.

Yazzo operates through a three-app ecosystem:

- Consumer app for discovery and reservation
- Partner app for publishing and company management
- Staff app for QR / 6-digit validation

The system closes the loop between publishing, booking, and on-site verification.

---

# 5. Business Model

Yazzo operates as a SaaS + marketplace model.

## Partner SaaS
€29.90 per month per company  
First 2 months free trial  

## Reservation Fee
Percentage-based fee tied to discount amount  

## Premium Users
€5.99 per month optional subscription  

This creates predictable recurring B2B revenue combined with scalable transaction upside.
